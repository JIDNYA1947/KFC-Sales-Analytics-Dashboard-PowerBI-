# 🍗 KFC Sales Analytics Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview
The **KFC Sales Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project designed to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights. This dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of revenue performance, product sales, customer behavior, and regional business trends, enabling data-driven decision-making through intuitive and interactive visualizations.

_____________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

## 🎯 Business Objectives
* Analyze overall sales performance and revenue growth.
* Identify top-performing products and categories.
* Understand customer purchasing behavior and preferences.
* Evaluate regional and city-wise business performance.
* Monitor payment methods and order preferences.
* Generate actionable insights to support business growth and operational efficiency.

## 📂 Dataset Information
The dataset used in this project was sourced from **Kaggle** and contains transactional sales data for a KFC restaurant business.

## 📈 Dashboard Pages
